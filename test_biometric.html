Test Instructions

  1. Check Device Detection: The device detection should show if your device is detected as mobile/tablet and if WebAuthn is supported.
  2. Enable Biometric: Click "Enable Biometric Authentication" to register your biometric (fingerprint/face ID).
  3. Test Authentication: Click "Test Biometric Login" to authenticate using your biometric.
  4. Test Popup: Click "Test Biometric Popup" to simulate the signup popup that appears after successful registration.
  5. Mobile/Tablet Only: The biometric icon should only appear in the email field on mobile/tablet devices in the main app.
  6. Signup Flow: In the main app, after completing signup (OTP or Google), the biometric popup should appear on mobile/tablet devices.
